EMAIL VERIFICATION - USER GUIDE AND TROUBLESHOOTING
=====================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Email verification is required for all new accounts and certain account changes. This guide explains the verification process and how to resolve common problems.

WHY EMAIL VERIFICATION IS REQUIRED
------------------------------------
- Confirms ownership of the email address
- Enables password reset and security notifications
- Required for compliance with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Prevents spam and fraudulent account creation

INITIAL ACCOUNT VERIFICATION
------------------------------
When you create a new account:
  1. A verification email is sent immediately to your registered address
  2. Subject line: "Confirm your YourSaaS Product account – action required"
  3. Click the "Confirm Email Address" button in the email
  4. Link expires after 48 hours

Account access before verification:
  - Read-only access to the dashboard
  - Cannot invite team members
  - Cannot upgrade subscription
  - Cannot use API keys
  - Full access unlocked after verification

RESENDING THE VERIFICATION EMAIL
----------------------------------
If you did not receive the verification email:
  1. Log in to your account (or go to the login page)
  2. You will see a yellow banner: "Please verify your email address"
  3. Click "Resend verification email"
  4. You may resend up to 5 times per hour

COMMON DELIVERY ISSUES
-----------------------

Problem: Email not received after 5 minutes
  Solutions:
    1. Check your spam/junk folder
    2. Search for sender: noreply@yoursaasproduct.com
    3. If using corporate email, ask your IT team to whitelist: *.yoursaasproduct.com
    4. Try adding noreply@yoursaasproduct.com to your contacts and resend
    5. If using Gmail, check the "Promotions" and "Updates" tabs

Problem: Verification link says "Link has expired"
  Solution: Request a new verification email. Links expire after 48 hours.

Problem: Verification link says "Link already used"
  Solution: Your email is already verified. Try logging in. If login fails, use Password Reset.

Problem: Verification link says "Invalid token"
  Cause: The link was corrupted, often by email clients that "wrap" URLs in tracking links
  Solution:
    1. Do not copy-paste the link manually
    2. Click directly on the button or hyperlink in the email
    3. If your email client breaks links, request a new verification email
    4. Contact support if the issue persists

CHANGING YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS
----------------------------
To change the email address on your account:
  1. Go to Account Settings → Profile
  2. Click "Change Email Address"
  3. Enter new email address
  4. A verification email is sent to BOTH the old and new address
  5. Click the link in the new email to confirm the change
  6. The old email receives a security notice

Note: You must have access to your current email to change it. If you have lost access, contact support with account ownership verification documents.

EMAIL DOMAIN RESTRICTIONS
--------------------------
The following email domains cannot be used for account registration:
  - Temporary/disposable email services (e.g., mailinator.com, guerrillamail.com)
  - Generic role addresses (e.g., admin@, info@, noreply@) are not recommended; verification emails may not be monitored

DELIVERABILITY REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------
If you are receiving verification emails but they go to spam:
  1. Mark the email as "Not Spam"
  2. Add noreply@yoursaasproduct.com as a contact
  3. Ask your IT/email admin to add to the allowlist:
     - SPF: include:_spf.yoursaasproduct.com
     - DKIM: yoursaasproduct.com._domainkey
     - Our sending IP range: 198.51.100.0/24

ENTERPRISE AND SSO USERS
--------------------------
If your organization uses Single Sign-On (SSO/SAML):
  - Email verification may be bypassed if your IdP confirms the email attribute
  - Contact your IT administrator to confirm the email attribute is correctly configured
  - The attribute name expected is: email or mail

SUPPORT ESCALATION
------------------
If email verification cannot be completed after all steps:
  - Contact support: support@yoursaasproduct.com
  - Include: account email, date/time of signup, email provider being used
  - A support agent can manually verify your email after identity confirmation
