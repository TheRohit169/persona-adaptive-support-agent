# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024

---

## Overview

YourSaaS Product aims to be fair and transparent about refunds. This policy outlines when you are eligible for a refund, how to request one, and what to expect.

---

## 1. Satisfaction Guarantee (30 Days)

New subscribers on the **Professional plan** are eligible for a full refund within **30 calendar days** of their first subscription purchase if they are not satisfied.

**Conditions:**
- Applies only to the first Professional plan purchase (not renewals or upgrades)
- Does not apply to the Starter (free) plan or add-ons purchased separately
- Does not apply if the account violated our Terms of Service during the period

**How to request:**
- Email **billing@yoursaasproduct.com** with subject: "Refund Request – 30-Day Guarantee"
- Include your account email and reason for cancellation (optional but appreciated)
- Refund processed within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method

---

## 2. Accidental Duplicate Charges

If you were charged twice for the same subscription period due to a system error:

- Contact **billing@yoursaasproduct.com** within **14 days** of the charge
- Include: account email, both transaction IDs or dates/amounts
- We will verify and refund the duplicate charge within **3–5 business days**

---

## 3. Failed Service During Billing Period

If we experience a significant service outage during your paid period:

- SLA credits are provided according to the SLA policy (see Service SLA document)
- SLA credits are the primary remedy for service disruptions
- Monetary refunds for outages are evaluated on a case-by-case basis for Enterprise customers

---

## 4. Annual Plan Cancellation

- **Within 30 days of annual renewal:** Full refund minus any service usage above the Starter plan limits
- **After 30 days:** No refund; subscription access continues until the annual period ends
- **Exception:** If we materially change the service in a way that prevents your intended use, contact us within 30 days of the change for a prorated refund

---

## 5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are **not eligible for refunds**:

- Monthly plan renewals (beyond the initial 30-day guarantee for new Professional subscriptions)
- Starter plan (free, no payment made)
- Usage-based add-on charges (API overage, storage overage)
- One-time professional services or setup fees
- Enterprise license fees after 30-day window
- Any charges more than 60 days old

---

## 6. Refund Process

1. Submit request to **billing@yoursaasproduct.com**
2. Our billing team reviews the request within **2 business days**
3. If approved, refund is initiated
4. Processing time:
   - Credit card: 5–7 business days to appear on statement
   - PayPal: 3–5 business days
   - Bank transfer / ACH: 7–10 business days
   - Wire transfer (Enterprise): 10–14 business days
5. Refund confirmation email sent when initiated

---

## 7. Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us **before** initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer.

- We respond to all billing disputes within 2 business days
- Unresolved chargebacks result in:
  - Immediate account suspension
  - Dispute filed with card network
  - Account marked as ineligible for future subscriptions until resolved
- If you have an open chargeback and wish to resolve it directly, email: **billing@yoursaasproduct.com**

---

## 8. Contact

| Purpose | Contact |
|---|---|
| General refund requests | billing@yoursaasproduct.com |
| Enterprise refund or credit | Your dedicated account manager |
| Urgent billing disputes | billing@yoursaasproduct.com (priority queue) |

Response time: **2 business days** for standard requests, **same business day** for Enterprise customers.
