# Billing Policy

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Applies To:** All YourSaaS Product Subscriptions

---

## 1. Billing Cycle

Subscriptions are billed on a **monthly or annual** basis, beginning on the date you first subscribe.

- **Monthly plans:** Billed on the same date each month (e.g., subscribed on the 15th → billed on the 15th of every month)
- **Annual plans:** Billed once per year on the anniversary of your subscription date
- **Invoice delivery:** Invoices are emailed to the billing email address on file within 24 hours of each charge

---

## 2. Payment Methods Accepted

- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards (where supported by card network)
- PayPal (available in 45+ countries)
- Bank transfer / ACH (US customers only, minimum $500 per transaction)
- SEPA Direct Debit (EU customers only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise customers only, minimum $5,000 per transaction)

---

## 3. Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

1. **Immediate:** Email notification sent to billing contact
2. **Day 3:** Automatic retry
3. **Day 7:** Second retry
4. **Day 14:** Final retry; account moves to **Past Due** status
5. **Day 21:** Account **suspended** (data retained for 30 days)
6. **Day 51:** Account **scheduled for deletion** if unpaid (final warning email sent)
7. **Day 60:** Account and data permanently deleted

To restore a suspended account, update your payment method in **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

---

## 4. Proration

When you **upgrade** your plan mid-cycle, you are charged the prorated difference immediately for the remaining days in the current billing period. Your next invoice will reflect the full new plan price.

When you **downgrade** your plan, the change takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. No partial refund is issued for the unused portion of the current period.

---

## 5. Taxes

- **US customers:** Sales tax applied where required by state law. Currently applies to: California, New York, Texas, Washington, and other applicable states.
- **EU customers:** VAT applied at the applicable rate for your country. Your VAT number can be entered in Settings → Billing to receive a VAT-exempt invoice (where eligible).
- **Other countries:** Local taxes may apply as required by law.

Tax amounts are shown on each invoice. Tax information cannot be retroactively changed for issued invoices.

---

## 6. Invoice Management

- Download invoices: **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**
- Update billing email: **Settings → Billing → Billing Contact**
- Update billing address: **Settings → Billing → Billing Details**
- Change tax information: **Settings → Billing → Tax Information**
- Invoices are retained for 7 years and available for download at any time

---

## 7. Subscription Cancellation

- **Monthly plans:** Cancel anytime; access continues until end of current billing period
- **Annual plans:** Cancel before renewal date; no mid-year cancellations or refunds except within the first 30 days (see Refund Policy)
- To cancel: **Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan**
- After cancellation, data is retained for 30 days before deletion begins

---

## 8. Disputed Charges

If you believe a charge is incorrect:

1. Review your invoice in **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**
2. If the charge appears to be an error, email **billing@yoursaasproduct.com** within **60 days** of the charge
3. Include: account email, invoice number, amount in question, and reason for dispute
4. We respond within 3 business days

> **Important:** Filing a chargeback with your bank without first contacting us may result in account suspension. Please contact us first so we can resolve the issue quickly.

---

## 9. Billing Contact

For billing inquiries:

- **Email:** billing@yoursaasproduct.com
- **Response time:** Within 2 business days
- **For urgent billing issues:** Live chat (Professional plan) or phone (Enterprise plan)

Enterprise customers should contact their dedicated account manager for billing matters.
