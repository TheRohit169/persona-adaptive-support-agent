SUBSCRIPTION UPGRADE AND PLAN MANAGEMENT GUIDE
===============================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This guide covers plan upgrades, downgrades, billing proration, and feature availability changes when switching subscription tiers.

AVAILABLE PLANS
---------------

Starter (Free):
  - Up to 3 team members
  - 5 GB storage
  - 100 API calls/minute
  - Community support only
  - Basic reporting
  - No SLA

Professional ($49/month or $490/year – save 17%):
  - Up to 20 team members
  - 50 GB storage
  - 1,000 API calls/minute
  - Priority email + chat support
  - Advanced reporting and analytics
  - 99.9% uptime SLA
  - Custom domain support
  - Audit logs (30-day retention)

Enterprise (Custom pricing):
  - Unlimited team members
  - Custom storage allocation
  - 10,000+ API calls/minute
  - 24/7 dedicated support
  - Custom SLA (up to 99.99%)
  - SSO/SAML integration
  - Audit logs (365-day retention)
  - Custom data residency options
  - Volume discounts
  - Dedicated infrastructure available

HOW TO UPGRADE YOUR PLAN
--------------------------
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to Settings → Subscription
  3. Click "Change Plan"
  4. Select the desired plan
  5. Review proration amount
  6. Confirm payment method
  7. Click "Upgrade Now"

New plan features are available immediately after upgrade.

UPGRADE PRORATION
-----------------
When upgrading mid-billing-cycle:
  - You pay only the prorated difference for the remaining days in the current cycle
  - Example: Upgrading from Starter to Professional on day 15 of a 30-day cycle
    Professional fee: $49/month
    Remaining days: 15/30 = 50%
    Prorated charge: $49 × 50% = $24.50 (charged immediately)
  - Your next full bill will be at the new plan rate

HOW TO DOWNGRADE YOUR PLAN
---------------------------
  1. Navigate to Settings → Subscription
  2. Click "Change Plan" → Select lower plan
  3. Confirm the downgrade

Downgrade notes:
  - Downgrade takes effect at the END of the current billing cycle
  - You retain current plan features until cycle end
  - No partial refund for unused time on the current plan
  - If current usage exceeds the new plan's limits, you must reduce usage before downgrade completes
  - Example: If you have 10 team members and downgrade to Starter (3 max), you must remove team members first

ANNUAL vs MONTHLY BILLING
---------------------------
  - Monthly: Pay month-to-month, cancel anytime
  - Annual: Pay once per year at 17% discount
  - Switching monthly → annual: Prorated credit applied; annual charge billed immediately
  - Switching annual → monthly: Takes effect at annual renewal date; no mid-year switch

ADD-ONS (Available for Professional and Enterprise)
----------------------------------------------------
  - Additional Storage: $10/month per 50 GB
  - Extra Team Members: $8/month per 5 additional seats
  - Extended Audit Logs: $15/month for 1-year retention (Professional)
  - Custom Domain: Included in Professional; additional domains $5/month each
  - API Overage: $0.0001 per request above monthly limit (auto-enabled if enabled in settings)

FEATURE CHANGES ON PLAN CHANGE
--------------------------------
When upgrading: New features enabled immediately
When downgrading:
  - Features remain accessible until end of cycle
  - Data within feature limits is retained
  - Data exceeding new limits is locked (not deleted) for 30 days
  - After 30 days, locked data may be deleted; export before downgrade

CANCELLATION
------------
  - Monthly plans: Cancel anytime; access until end of billing period
  - Annual plans: Cancel at renewal date; no mid-year refunds except in first 30 days
  - 30-day money-back guarantee on first Professional subscription purchase
  - To cancel: Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan
