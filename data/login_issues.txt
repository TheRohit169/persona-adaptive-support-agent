LOGIN ISSUES - TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
=====================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Login problems are among the most common support requests. This guide covers all known login failure scenarios and their resolutions.

COMMON LOGIN ERROR CODES
------------------------
- ERR_AUTH_001: Invalid credentials (username/password mismatch)
- ERR_AUTH_002: Account locked after 5 failed attempts
- ERR_AUTH_003: Session token expired
- ERR_AUTH_004: IP address blocked (suspicious activity)
- ERR_AUTH_005: Account not yet verified
- ERR_AUTH_006: Multi-factor authentication required but not configured
- ERR_AUTH_007: OAuth provider connection failure
- ERR_AUTH_008: SAML assertion validation failed (SSO users)

STEP-BY-STEP TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------------------

Step 1: Verify Credentials
- Confirm you are using the correct email address associated with your account
- Check for accidental spaces before or after the email/password
- Ensure Caps Lock is not enabled
- Try copying and pasting the password from a password manager

Step 2: Check Account Lock Status
- After 5 failed login attempts, the account is temporarily locked for 30 minutes
- Locked accounts receive an email notification with an unlock link
- Wait 30 minutes and retry, or use the unlock link in your email
- Contact support if you did not receive the unlock email

Step 3: Clear Browser Cache and Cookies
- Stale cookies or corrupted cache files often cause persistent login failures
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data → Cookies and Cache
- Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All
- After clearing, close all browser tabs and retry login

Step 4: Try a Different Browser or Incognito Mode
- Open an incognito/private window and attempt login
- If login succeeds in incognito, a browser extension is interfering
- Disable extensions one by one to identify the conflict

Step 5: Check for System Outages
- Visit https://status.yoursaasproduct.com to check for ongoing incidents
- During outages, login services may be unavailable platform-wide

Step 6: Reset Your Password
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter your registered email address
- Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the reset email
- Password reset links expire after 24 hours

Step 7: Check Network/Firewall
- Corporate firewalls may block certain authentication endpoints
- Whitelist the following domains: auth.yoursaasproduct.com, api.yoursaasproduct.com
- VPN configurations can conflict with geolocation-based security checks
- Try disabling VPN temporarily to test

ACCOUNT LOCK POLICY
-------------------
- 5 failed attempts in 10 minutes = 30-minute automatic lock
- 10 failed attempts in 1 hour = 24-hour lock requiring admin unlock
- 20 failed attempts = permanent lock requiring identity verification

CONTACT SUPPORT
---------------
If none of the above steps resolve your issue:
- Email: support@yoursaasproduct.com
- Live Chat: Available Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM UTC
- Include your account email, error code (if shown), and browser/OS details
