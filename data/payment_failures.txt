PAYMENT FAILURES - TROUBLESHOOTING AND RESOLUTION GUIDE
========================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Payment processing failures can occur at multiple points in the transaction pipeline. This document covers error types, root causes, and resolution procedures for users and support agents.

PAYMENT ERROR CODES
-------------------
- PAY_001: Card declined by issuing bank
- PAY_002: Insufficient funds
- PAY_003: Card expired
- PAY_004: Invalid CVV/security code
- PAY_005: Billing address mismatch (AVS failure)
- PAY_006: Card velocity limit exceeded
- PAY_007: Suspected fraud – transaction blocked by fraud engine
- PAY_008: Payment gateway timeout (retry safe)
- PAY_009: Currency not supported in your region
- PAY_010: 3D Secure authentication failed
- PAY_011: Card not enrolled in 3D Secure

IMMEDIATE STEPS FOR USERS
--------------------------

Step 1: Verify Card Details
- Re-enter card number, expiry date, and CVV carefully
- Ensure billing address exactly matches what is registered with your bank
- Check that your card has not expired

Step 2: Contact Your Bank
- The most common cause of payment failure is a block placed by the issuing bank
- Call the number on the back of your card and ask them to authorize the transaction
- Mention the merchant name: YourSaaS Product Inc.
- Ask them to whitelist future recurring charges

Step 3: Try a Different Payment Method
- Use a different credit or debit card
- Use PayPal (available in 45+ countries)
- Use bank transfer / ACH (US customers only, 3–5 business day processing)
- Use SEPA Direct Debit (EU customers only)

Step 4: Check for 3D Secure Prompts
- Some transactions require OTP (One-Time Password) verification via SMS
- Ensure your mobile number is current with your bank
- Complete the verification within 5 minutes before the session expires

Step 5: Disable VPN
- VPN usage can trigger fraud detection if the transaction IP mismatches the billing country
- Disable VPN and retry the transaction

RETRY POLICY
------------
- Automatic retry: Failed subscription payments are retried on days 3, 7, and 14
- If all retries fail, the account moves to "Past Due" status
- Past Due accounts retain access for 7 days, after which access is suspended
- To restore access, update payment method in Account Settings → Billing

REFUND POLICY SUMMARY
---------------------
- Accidental duplicate charges are refunded within 5–7 business days
- For refund requests, see the Refund Policy document
- All refund requests must go through the billing team

FRAUD BLOCKS
------------
- If PAY_007 appears, the transaction has been flagged by the automated fraud system
- This cannot be resolved by support agents – it requires manual fraud team review
- Submit a support ticket with subject line: "PAY_007 Fraud Block – [your account email]"
- Resolution time: 2–4 business hours

AGENT ESCALATION TRIGGERS
--------------------------
- Customer reports PAY_007 and bank confirms no block on their end
- Customer has updated payment method but subscription is still suspended
- Customer reports being charged but not receiving access
- Any chargeback or dispute filed through the bank
