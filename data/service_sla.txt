SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) - SUMMARY DOCUMENT
==================================================

EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 2024
APPLIES TO: Professional and Enterprise Plans

1. UPTIME COMMITMENT
--------------------
YourSaaS Product commits to the following monthly uptime percentages:

  Professional Plan: 99.9% uptime
    - Maximum downtime: 43 minutes and 49 seconds per month
    - Credit: 10% per 0.1% below target, up to 30% monthly credit

  Enterprise Plan: 99.95% uptime
    - Maximum downtime: 21 minutes and 54 seconds per month
    - Credit: 15% per 0.05% below target, up to 50% monthly credit
    - Includes optional dedicated infrastructure with 99.99% SLA available

Starter Plan: Best-effort, no SLA commitment.

2. RESPONSE TIME SLA
--------------------
  - P0 (Critical): First response within 15 minutes (Enterprise); 30 minutes (Professional)
  - P1 (High): First response within 1 hour (Enterprise); 4 hours (Professional)
  - P2 (Medium): First response within 8 hours (all plans)
  - P3 (Low): First response within 2 business days (all plans)

Support hours:
  - Starter: Business hours only (Mon–Fri, 09:00–18:00 UTC)
  - Professional: Extended hours (Mon–Fri, 06:00–22:00 UTC)
  - Enterprise: 24/7/365 support with dedicated account manager

3. PERFORMANCE SLA
------------------
  - API response time: 95th percentile < 500ms for standard endpoints
  - Dashboard load time: < 2 seconds for 95% of page loads
  - Data processing latency: < 60 seconds for standard reports

4. WHAT IS EXCLUDED FROM SLA
-----------------------------
The SLA does not apply to downtime caused by:
  - Customer configuration errors or misuse of the API
  - Third-party service failures outside our control
  - Force majeure events (natural disasters, government actions, etc.)
  - Scheduled maintenance windows (communicated in advance per policy)
  - Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS) attacks
  - User-initiated actions (e.g., account deletion, rate limit breaches)

5. HOW DOWNTIME IS CALCULATED
------------------------------
  - Calculated per calendar month
  - Measured from public monitoring data at https://status.yoursaasproduct.com
  - "Downtime" = complete inability to access core features (login, dashboard, API)
  - Performance degradation alone does not count unless agreed separately in Enterprise SLA

6. SLA CREDIT PROCESS
----------------------
  - Credits applied automatically within 5 business days of month close
  - If automatic credit not received, submit claim within 30 days of incident
  - Claims: billing@yoursaasproduct.com with subject "SLA Credit Request – [Month Year]"
  - Credits are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash
  - Credits capped at the monthly subscription fee for the affected month

7. ENTERPRISE CUSTOM SLA
-------------------------
Enterprise customers may negotiate custom SLA terms including:
  - Higher uptime commitments (up to 99.99%)
  - Custom response time requirements
  - Dedicated infrastructure options
  - Financial penalties in addition to service credits
  - Custom maintenance windows

Contact your account manager to discuss custom SLA arrangements.

8. DATA RETENTION SLA
----------------------
  - User data retained for the duration of the subscription
  - 30-day data export window after account cancellation
  - Backups retained for 90 days (Enterprise: 365 days)
  - Data deletion completed within 30 days of account closure

9. SUPPORT CHANNELS BY PLAN
----------------------------
  Starter:
    - Email support only
    - Community forums
    - Self-service knowledge base

  Professional:
    - Email support
    - Live chat (business hours)
    - Priority queue (faster response than Starter)

  Enterprise:
    - All Professional channels
    - Phone support (24/7)
    - Dedicated account manager
    - Slack Connect channel option
    - Quarterly business reviews
    - Early access to new features
